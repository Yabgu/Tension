# Technical Reasoning

## Why Zig Core
- Manual memory control, no hidden allocations.
- Direct C interop (SDL2, OpenGL, OpenAL).
- Less compiler friction than Rust, safer than C.

## Why TypeScript (AssemblyScript) Modules
- Familiar syntax for web-native devs.
- Easy to compile to WASM.
- Great for rapid prototyping and modding.

## Why WASM
- Language-agnostic: Rust, C, TS, Grain all possible.
- Sandboxed execution.
- Hot-reloadable modules.

## Comparison
| Feature | C++ | Rust | Zig |
|---------|-----|------|-----|
| Safety | Low | High | Medium |
| Complexity | High | High | Low |
| WASM Support | Manual | Excellent | Excellent |
| Build Friction | High | High | Low |

VISION (WASM / Frankenstein Fork)

- Core Philosophy:
  The engine is not just code, but a runtime. Native core is skeletal; all gameplay logic lives in WebAssembly modules.
  WASM is the universal runtime: portable, sandboxed, future-proof.

- Long-Term Goal:
  A hybrid engine where rendering, physics, and audio are native, but everything else (AI, gameplay, UI, procedural generation) is WASM-driven.
  Hot-reloadable modules, modding-first design, and deterministic execution.

- Values:
  • Portability across desktop, mobile, and browser.
  • Sandbox safety and deterministic behavior.
  • Modularity: every system is replaceable.
  • Future-proofing over short-term convenience.
  • Brutal honesty: no fluff, no editor bloat, only systems that matter.

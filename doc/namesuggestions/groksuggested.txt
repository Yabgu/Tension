### Functional
- **WeaveCore**: This name captures the seamless weaving of native rendering cores with WASM-based gameplay modules into a modular, replaceable framework.
- **BindForge**: It emphasizes the forging and binding of disparate systems into a cohesive, sandboxed toolset that prioritizes modularity and connectivity.
- **SysWeft**: Drawing from weaving terminology, it highlights the intricate threading of minimalist systems to create a robust, architecture-driven platform.

### Philosophical
- **BrutForge**: This evokes a brutal, no-frills forging process that aligns with the honest, risk-embracing philosophy rejecting editor bloat for raw systems design.
- **RawNexus**: It suggests an unpolished, authentic nexus point where developers connect honest components to enable dangerous, novel creations.
- **CandidCore**: Reflecting candid transparency, it underscores a core-focused toolset that appeals to developers seeking truthful, unbounded innovation without misleading features.

### Evocative
- **VanguardWeave**: This implies a vanguard of future-proof weaving, positioning the toolset at the forefront of WASM-native innovation for unique architectures.
- **HorizonBind**: It conveys binding expansive horizons, symbolizing the connection of forward-thinking systems to unlock truly unbounded, cutting-edge developments.
- **NovaSys**: Suggesting a nova-like burst of new systems, it hints at explosive innovation and authenticity in a minimalist platform built for risky, novel pursuits.

My project is positioned as the first serious WASM-native game framework.

Misleading: The name should reflect that this is a minimalist, systems-driven toolset, not a bloated, all-in-one game engine.

Modularity & Connection: The idea of connecting disparate parts into a cohesive whole.

Future-Proofing & Innovation: A name that suggests it's on the cutting edge and built for the future.

Systems & Architecture: A name that hints at its minimalist, systems-driven design.

Authenticity & Honesty: A name that reflects the manifesto's 'brutal honesty' and appeals to developers who 'crave risk, novelty, and danger.'

Please provide a list of 5-10 original name suggestions, organized by category (e.g., Functional, Philosophical, Evocative). For each name, provide a one-sentence explanation of why it fits the project's vision. Ensure the names are unique and not already widely used in the game development space.

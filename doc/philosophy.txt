PHILOSOPHY (WASM / Tension Fork)

- Ethos:
  Playing safe is the real trap. Risk is survival.
  This engine exists to embrace the forbidden lamp: dangerous, alluring, future-proof.

- Contrast with SDL2 Fork:
  • SDL2 fork: control, tradition, legacy.
  • WASM fork: modularity, portability, revolution.

- Manifesto:
  • No editor bloat, no hand-holding.
  • Brutal systems-first design.
  • Every module replaceable, every system sandboxed.
  • Engine as platform, not product.
  • Attract the devs who crave risk, novelty, and danger.
